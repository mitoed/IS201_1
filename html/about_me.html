Education History

I started school wanting to become an engineer. I was able to intern at Orenco Systems Inc. for a few summers and found that I really enjoyed process improvement and business management even more than the engineering work I did. So, I decided to pursue an education in business, where I discovered a great love for financial analysis and accounting.

With that excitement, I have prepared to enter the Accounting program at BYU, and, to further my hands-on learning, I built an Excel spreadsheet to forecast my own family finances for the next few years. This creation led to sharing the benefits with others, who expressed interest in having a copy for themselves. I have since played with a number of versions and recently shared a completed version in order to get feedback for future development of a desktop application. I do this all in hopes that it will be of help to many fellow college students who too often struggle with their new financial responsibilities.
